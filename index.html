What is Personal Training?

According to NASM, personal training is a service where a professional provides individuals with information, motivation, and guidance to improve health and meet fitness goals!

Personal training is much more then just having a workout partner. It is having a profession who is educated in health and fitness to help you reach your goals. The personal trainers have gotten their certifications through NASM and other resources just as respected.

Why Personal Training Interests Me!

Ever since I was young, I have always been active, whether that be through playing baseball, basketball, soccer, or going to the gym. Being active and going to the gym has helped me a bunch. Not just with sports, there are a ton of mental benefits as well. I have experienced this for myself! I have been working out seriously for about 4 years now and have learned a lot through the process. I wanted to give people the same benefits I have experienced through fitness, so over the summer, I decided to get my personal training certification through NASM. I am super passionate about fitness and helping others, so I thought this would be a great way to combine the two!
